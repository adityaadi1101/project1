Lalit Narain Tirhut College

Lalit Narain Tirhut Mahavidyalaya is a prestigious co-educational college, located at Aghoriya Bazar chowk, a prime location of Muzaffarpur. The college was established in the year 1967, named Tirhut Mahavidyalaya by the vigorous effort of it founder members, under the leadership of the then Railway Minister, great visionary.
